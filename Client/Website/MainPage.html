About Valor

Valor was created with one mission: to fight for those who’ve already fought for us. Valor is a safe space built just for them, where they can track their emotions, reflect through journaling, and reconnect with purpose. It’s more than a platform—it’s a lifeline.

Features

  • Mood Tracking - The ability to click 8 pre-selected buttons to capture your emotions.
  • Personalized Affirmations - Each pre-selected mood comes with personal Bible quotes.
  • Journaling - A calming space to heal, reflect, and release your thoughts.
